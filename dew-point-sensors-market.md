<p><ul><li>Capacitance Method</li><li>Resistance Method</li><li>Cold Mirror Method</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dew point sensors are crucial for measuring moisture levels in various industries. The capacitance method detects changes in dielectric constant due to moisture, providing high accuracy in humid environments. The resistance method measures moisture through changes in electrical resistance, suitable for lower moisture applications. The cold mirror method uses a chilled surface to determine dew point by measuring condensation, offering reliable results. Other methods include chilled mirror technology and optical sensors, catering to specific industrial needs with varied sensitivity and response times.</p></p>
