<p><strong>The Marine Navigation Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Lights</li><li>All-Round Lights</li><li>Stern Lights</li><li>Masthead Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine navigation lighting comprises various types essential for safe maritime operation. Side lights, positioned on both sides of a vessel, indicate its direction and presence at night. All-round lights provide visibility from all angles, typically used on anchored or moored vessels. Stern lights are located at the rear, signaling the vessel's position to others behind it. Masthead lights, mounted high on the mast, signify the vessel's presence and heading, crucial for preventing collisions and ensuring safe navigation.</p></p>
