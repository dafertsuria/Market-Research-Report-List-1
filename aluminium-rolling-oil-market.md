<p><strong>The Aluminium Rolling Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Rolling</li><li>Hot Rolling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aluminium Rolling Oil market is segmented into two main types based on the rolling process: cold rolling and hot rolling. Cold rolling involves the deformation of aluminium at room temperature, enhancing strength and surface finish, and typically requires higher viscosity oils for effective lubrication and cooling. In contrast, hot rolling occurs at elevated temperatures, allowing for easier shaping and reduced energy consumption, with lighter oils utilized to withstand the heat and prevent oxidation. Each type caters to specific industrial needs in aluminium production.</p></p>
