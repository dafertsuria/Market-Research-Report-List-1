<p><p>Passive chilled beams are innovative heating and cooling systems that utilize the principle of convection to regulate temperature in commercial and industrial buildings. They consist of water-filled coils that absorb heat from the indoor air, allowing for effective climate control without the need for fans. This system operates quietly, is energy-efficient, and reduces overall energy consumption, making it increasingly popular in modern building designs.</p><p>The Passive Chilled Beams Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.4% during the forecast period, fueled by rising demand for energy-efficient HVAC systems and increasing awareness of sustainable building practices. Additionally, advancements in technology are enabling better integration of chilled beams with smart building systems, enhancing their appeal. Key sectors contributing to this growth include commercial, educational, and healthcare facilities, where comfortable indoor environments are crucial. As regulations around energy efficiency become more stringent, the adoption of passive chilled beam systems is set to accelerate, positioning them as a key component in the future of building climate control solutions.</p></p>
