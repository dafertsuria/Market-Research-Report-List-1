<p><p>The fused silica fiber bundle market encompasses two primary types: single branch fiber bundles and multi-branch fiber bundles. Single branch fiber bundles consist of a single, continuous strand, providing a straightforward solution for applications requiring high transmission efficiency and minimal signal loss. In contrast, multi-branch fiber bundles feature multiple strands stemming from a central point, enabling the distribution of light or signals across various channels, enhancing flexibility for complex applications such as endoscopy and illumination systems. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
