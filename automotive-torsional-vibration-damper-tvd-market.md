<p><p>The Automotive Torsional Vibration Damper (TVD) market includes various types designed to mitigate vibrations in vehicle drivetrains. Clutch-type dampers utilize friction to absorb vibrations, enhancing performance and longevity. Dual Mass Flywheel (DMF) types feature two separate masses connected by springs, effectively isolating engine vibrations from the transmission. Other types may include viscous or pendulum dampers, each tailored to specific applications, helping to improve ride comfort, reduce noise, and enhance overall vehicle performance in diverse automotive environments.</p></p>
