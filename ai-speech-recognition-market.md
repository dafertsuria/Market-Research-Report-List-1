<p><ul><li>Automatic Speech Recognition</li><li>Text to Speech</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AI speech recognition market includes two main types: Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) and Text to Speech (TTS). ASR technology converts spoken language into text, enabling applications such as voice assistants, transcription services, and voice commands. In contrast, TTS transforms text into spoken words, facilitating accessibility for those with visual impairments and enhancing user engagement in applications like virtual assistants and audiobooks. Together, these technologies enhance communication efficiency and offer innovative solutions across various industries.</p></p>
