<p><p>The Electroencephalogram (EEG) device market comprises several types, each serving specific diagnostic and monitoring needs. Conventional EEG devices capture electrical brain activity using scalp electrodes for routine assessments of neurological conditions. Video EEG systems combine EEG recording with simultaneous video monitoring, enhancing the diagnosis of seizures and other disorders by providing context to brain activity. Dynamic EEG devices offer continuous monitoring over extended periods, ideal for tracking changes in brain function, particularly in critical care settings or during sleep studies.</p></p>
