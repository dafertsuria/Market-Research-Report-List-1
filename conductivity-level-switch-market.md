<p><strong>The Conductivity Level Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Electrode</li><li>Suspended Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Conductivity Level Switch market comprises two primary types: Solid Electrode and Suspended Electrode switches. Solid Electrode switches utilize fixed probes to detect liquid levels by measuring conductivity variations directly, making them ideal for consistent fluid environments. In contrast, Suspended Electrode switches employ flexible probes suspended in the liquid, allowing for detection of level changes without being fully submerged. This versatility makes them suitable for various applications, particularly in environments with fluctuating levels or where space constraints exist.</p></p>
