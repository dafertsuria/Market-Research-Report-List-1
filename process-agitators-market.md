<p><p>Process agitators are crucial in various industries for mixing and blending fluids. The market can be categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Top-entry** agitators, ideal for large tanks, mix from above. </p><p>2. **Side-entry** agitators are used for horizontal vessels to minimize contamination. </p><p>3. **Bottom-entry** agitators, effective in deep tanks, ensure thorough mixing from below. </p><p>4. **Portable** agitators allow flexibility in use across multiple locations. </p><p>5. **Static** agitators, featuring fixed elements, enhance flow without moving parts, ideal for certain applications.</p></p>
