<p><p>The Modular Raman Spectrometer market comprises two main types: Direct Coupling Modular Raman Spectrometers and Fiber Optic Modular Raman Spectrometers. Direct Coupling systems allow for direct connection to the sample, providing high sensitivity and quick analysis, suitable for laboratory settings. In contrast, Fiber Optic Modular Raman Spectrometers use fiber optic cables to remotely access samples, enabling flexibility and ease of use in diverse environments, such as field applications or hazardous areas. Both types enhance analytical capabilities in various industries.</p></p>
