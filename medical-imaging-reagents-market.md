<p><ul><li>Contrast Reagents</li><li>Optical Reagents</li><li>Nuclear Reagents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical imaging reagents market comprises three primary types: contrast reagents, optical reagents, and nuclear reagents. Contrast reagents enhance the visibility of internal structures during imaging procedures, commonly used in X-rays and MRIs. Optical reagents, often employed in techniques like fluorescence imaging, aid in visualizing cellular and molecular processes. Nuclear reagents, including radionuclides, are utilized in nuclear medicine for diagnosis and therapy, facilitating the identification of diseases through imaging modalities like PET and SPECT scans.</p></p>
