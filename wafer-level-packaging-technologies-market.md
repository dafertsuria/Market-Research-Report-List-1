<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wafer Level Packaging Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging</li><li>Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer Level Packaging (WLP) technologies are crucial in the semiconductor industry for enhancing device performance and miniaturization. Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging involves integrating chips directly onto the wafer, utilizing smaller bond pads, which simplifies the packaging process but limits the I/O density. In contrast, Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging enables a larger number of I/Os by redistributing the connections beyond the wafer's edges, supporting more complex designs. Both methods aim to reduce size, improve thermal performance, and increase manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
