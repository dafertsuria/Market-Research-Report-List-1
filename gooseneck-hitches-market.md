<p><p>Gooseneck hitches come in two primary market types: above-bed and under-bed installations. Above-bed installations involve a visible hitch that sits on the truck bed's surface, allowing for easy access and quick setup. In contrast, under-bed installations are discreet, with the hitch components hidden beneath the truck bed when not in use, providing a clean appearance and full bed access. Both types cater to different user preferences in functionality and aesthetics, enhancing towing capabilities for various applications.</p></p>
