<p><p>Allergy relieving eye drops are categorized into several types based on their active ingredients. Antihistamines block histamine receptors to alleviate itching and redness. Non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) reduce inflammation and discomfort. Vasoconstrictors work by narrowing blood vessels to minimize redness and swelling. Additionally, other formulations may combine these actions or include lubricants to provide moisture and comfort. Each type serves distinct purposes in managing allergy symptoms, making them essential for individuals experiencing allergic conjunctivitis or seasonal allergies.</p></p>
