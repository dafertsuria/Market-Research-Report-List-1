<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Multipoint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) market can be categorized into single point and multipoint systems. Single point sensing focuses on measuring temperature at specific locations, offering simplicity and lower costs, suitable for applications with fewer measurement needs. In contrast, multipoint sensing utilizes fiber optic cables to monitor temperature continuously along extended lengths, enabling detailed thermal profiling. This technology is ideal for complex environments, such as pipelines or power generation, where comprehensive temperature data is crucial for operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
