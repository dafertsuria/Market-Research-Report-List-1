<p><p>Level B suits are designed to protect against chemical hazards while allowing some degree of breathability. The market is divided into two types: Fully Encapsulated and Partially Encapsulating suits. Fully Encapsulated suits provide complete coverage, protecting the wearer from external contaminants and containing a self-contained breathing apparatus. In contrast, Partially Encapsulating suits offer some respiratory protection but allow for more mobility and flexibility, making them ideal for scenarios with lower chemical exposure risks. Both types cater to specific safety needs in hazardous environments.</p></p>
