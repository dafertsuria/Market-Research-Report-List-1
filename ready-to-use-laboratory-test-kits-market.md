<p><p>The ready-to-use laboratory test kits market encompasses various types of assays designed for rapid diagnostics. Agglutination assays involve the clumping of particles for detection. Immune-chromatographic techniques use antibodies in a strip format to visualize results. Immune-dot techniques feature lines or dots for antigen detection. Immune-filtration techniques separate analytes through a filter, while flow-through methods allow sample flow along a membrane. Lateral flow tests offer quick results along a defined path, and solid-phase assays immobilize antigens on a surface for evaluation.</p></p>
