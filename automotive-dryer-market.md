<p><p>The automotive dryer market comprises various dryer types designed for efficient drying processes in the automotive industry. Rotary dryers utilize a rotating drum for continuous material processing. Dispersion dryers enhance drying by promoting a thin layer of material. Fluid bed dryers use hot air to suspend and dry particles uniformly, while belt dryers transport materials on a moving belt through heated chambers. Other dryer types include vacuum and microwave dryers, offering specialized solutions for unique automotive applications.</p></p>
