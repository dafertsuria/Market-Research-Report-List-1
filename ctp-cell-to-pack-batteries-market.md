<p><p>The CTP (Cell to Pack) battery market primarily features two types: Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) batteries and Ternary Lithium-ion batteries. LFP batteries are known for their safety, thermal stability, and longevity, making them suitable for applications like electric vehicles. Ternary Li-ion batteries, composed of nickel, cobalt, and manganese, provide higher energy density and efficiency, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Both technologies aim to increase energy storage efficiency and reduce manufacturing complexity, enhancing overall battery performance.</p></p>
