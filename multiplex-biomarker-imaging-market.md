<p><p>The multiplex biomarker imaging market encompasses various assay types that enhance the analysis of tissue samples. Immunohistochemistry (IHC) assays use antibodies to detect specific proteins, providing insights into cellular characteristics. Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) assays visualize genetic material by using fluorescent probes, allowing for the detection of chromosomal abnormalities. Tissue Microarray (TMA) assays enable the simultaneous analysis of multiple tissue samples on a single slide, facilitating high-throughput biomarker studies. Together, these techniques advance diagnostic and therapeutic applications in personalized medicine.</p></p>
