<p><p>The arrhythmia management market encompasses several key types of devices. Pacemakers are implanted to regulate heartbeats in patients with bradycardia, ensuring consistent heart rhythm. Defibrillators, including implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs), are used to correct life-threatening arrhythmias through shock therapy. Ablation devices target and eliminate abnormal electrical pathways in the heart, providing a non-invasive solution for various arrhythmia types. Together, these technologies enhance patient outcomes by offering tailored treatment options for cardiac rhythm disorders.</p></p>
