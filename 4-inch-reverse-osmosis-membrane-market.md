<p><p>The 4-inch reverse osmosis membrane market features two primary types: composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes are designed with multiple layers, often enhancing filtration efficiency and chemical resistance, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, asymmetric membranes have a uniform structure, characterized by a thin selective layer atop a porous support, providing strength and durability. Both types are crucial in water purification systems, catering to diverse needs in industries such as residential water treatment, food and beverage processing, and wastewater management.</p></p>
