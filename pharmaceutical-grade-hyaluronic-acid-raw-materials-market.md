<p><p>The pharmaceutical-grade hyaluronic acid raw materials market is primarily categorized into two types: microbial fermentation and animal tissue. This method caters to the growing demand for vegan and sustainable options. Both types serve critical applications in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics.</p></p>
