<p><p>The milk protein isolate market primarily comprises two main types: casein and whey. Casein, the dominant protein in milk, is known for its slow digestion rate, making it ideal for muscle recovery and sustained amino acid release. Whey protein, on the other hand, is quickly absorbed, popular for post-workout nutrition due to its high leucine content, which aids muscle growth. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, from athletes to health-conscious individuals seeking dietary supplements and functional foods.</p></p>
