<p><ul><li>Direct</li><li>Indirect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal management EV fluid market encompasses two primary types: direct and indirect systems. Direct thermal management involves the fluids being in direct contact with the components needing temperature regulation, allowing for efficient heat transfer. In contrast, indirect thermal management utilizes a separate loop or heat exchanger system to manage temperatures, protecting sensitive components while ensuring optimal performance. Both approaches are crucial for maintaining battery efficiency and longevity, ultimately enhancing electric vehicle performance and safety.</p></p>
