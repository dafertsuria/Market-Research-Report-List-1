<p><p>The UV light sensors market is categorized into three types based on the wavelength of ultraviolet light: UVA, UVB, and UVC sensors. UVA sensors, detecting wavelengths from 320 to 400 nm, are commonly used in applications like skin protection and tanning. UVB sensors cover 280 to 320 nm, focusing on applications related to sunburn risk and indoor tanning equipment. UVC sensors, which capture wavelengths from 100 to 280 nm, are primarily utilized for germicidal purposes and water purification systems. Each type serves specific applications tailored to their wavelength ranges.</p></p>
