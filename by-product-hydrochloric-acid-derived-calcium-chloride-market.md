<p><p>The by-product hydrochloric acid derived calcium chloride market consists of two main types: Calcium Chloride Dihydrate and Calcium Chloride Anhydrous. Calcium Chloride Dihydrate contains two water molecules per calcium chloride formula unit, often used in de-icing and dust control due to its high solubility. In contrast, Calcium Chloride Anhydrous is devoid of water, making it a highly effective drying agent and a key component in various industrial applications. Both types are derived from the process of utilizing hydrochloric acid as a by-product.</p></p>
