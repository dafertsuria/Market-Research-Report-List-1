<p><p>The lung biopsy systems market comprises several key types. Needle biopsy involves using a thin needle to extract tissue samples from the lungs, often guided by imaging techniques. Thoracoscopic biopsy is a minimally invasive surgical procedure where a camera and instruments are inserted through a small chest incision to obtain lung tissue. Transbronchial biopsy is performed through a bronchoscope inserted into the airway, allowing for targeted sampling of lung lesions. Each method offers distinct advantages based on the clinical scenario and patient's condition.</p></p>
