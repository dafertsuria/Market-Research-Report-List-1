<p><p>Long-term mechanical circulatory support (LT-MCS) encompasses devices that assist patients with severe heart failure. The Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) device provides oxygenation and circulatory support by oxygenating blood outside the body, often used in critical care settings. Extracorporeal Ventricular Assist Devices (E-VADs) help maintain heart function by mechanically assisting the ventricles. Other devices include total artificial hearts and specialized pumps designed for long-term use. Together, these technologies improve patient outcomes and quality of life for those with advanced heart conditions.</p></p>
