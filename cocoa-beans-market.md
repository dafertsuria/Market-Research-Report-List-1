<p><ul><li>Criollo Cocoa Beans</li><li>Forastero Cocoa Beans</li><li>Trinitario Cocoa Beans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cocoa beans market is primarily divided into three types: Criollo, Forastero, and Trinitario. Criollo beans are rare, known for their complex flavors and high-quality chocolate, making them highly sought after and expensive. Forastero beans represent the bulk of global production, valued for their hardiness and higher yields, typically used in mass-market chocolate. Trinitario beans are a hybrid of Criollo and Forastero, combining desirable traits of both, offering unique flavor profiles while being more resilient than Criollo.</p></p>
