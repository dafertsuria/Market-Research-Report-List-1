<p><p>The centralized vacuum system market encompasses various types, primarily classified into pipeline and built-in systems. Pipeline systems consist of a network of tubes installed throughout a building, allowing for efficient dirt collection via a central unit. Built-in systems, on the other hand, integrate vacuum components directly into walls or cabinetry, offering a streamlined design and convenience for users. Both types provide effective cleaning solutions while enhancing the aesthetic appeal of spaces, catering to residential and commercial needs.</p></p>
