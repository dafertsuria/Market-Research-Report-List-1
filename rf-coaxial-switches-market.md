<p><p>RF coaxial switches are essential components in telecommunications and broadcasting, allowing for efficient signal routing. The market consists of several types: SPnT (Single Pole, n-Throw) switches, which can connect one input to multiple outputs; SPDT (Single Pole, Double Throw) switches, enabling connection between one input and two outputs; DPDT (Double Pole, Double Throw) switches, which connect two inputs to two outputs; and other variants, accommodating specific applications. These selections help optimize signal distribution across RF systems.</p></p>
