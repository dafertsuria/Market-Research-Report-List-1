<p><p>The inhalational anesthesia drugs market features several key agents, including Sevoflurane, Desflurane, Isoflurane, and Enflurane. Sevoflurane is favored for its rapid onset and recovery, making it suitable for outpatient procedures. Desflurane offers quick elimination and is often used in longer surgeries. Isoflurane, with its low blood solubility, provides stable hemodynamics and is widely used in various surgeries. Enflurane, although less common today, is known for its analgesic properties. Each agent caters to specific clinical needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
