<p><strong>The Surface Retarders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surface retarders market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based surface retarders utilize water as a primary solvent, making them environmentally friendly and low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs). They offer ease of application and quick drying times. In contrast, solvent-based surface retarders use organic solvents and provide strong adhesion and durability, often performing better in harsh weather conditions. Each type serves different project requirements, with water-based options favored for sustainability and solvent-based options for robustness.</p></p>
